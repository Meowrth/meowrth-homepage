The Purrfect Meme Coin on Solana

Meowrth [MEWR] is a community-driven meme coin built on the Solana blockchain.
Among the many planets in the solar system, Mars was too cold and too quiet for cats.
Memes are born in communities — but there are no memes on Mars. So they made a decision:
“Let’s go to Earth. Let’s go to Meowrth!” Based on the storyline of “A Martian cat’s journey to settle on Earth,”
Meowrth aims to build a creator-friendly meme ecosystem, a community-led token economy, and a fun, culturally connected world.
